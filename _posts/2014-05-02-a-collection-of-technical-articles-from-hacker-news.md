## Technical Articles

### Intro

In the last 5 months that I have been reading Hacker News, I have put every technical article that I found interesting into my Evernote collection. While I suspect that I will never get through this massive backlog of articles, I hope that posting them will provide a trove of resources for anyone else interested in researching new computer science topics.

The articles are organized in the same manner as those in my Evernote folder. There are 4 broad topics, each with many sub-topics full of articles pertaining to that subject.
